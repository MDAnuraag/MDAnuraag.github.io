Short notes on inference errors I have encountered in my own work.

---

[Good fits don't imply identifiability](/notes/good-fits/)  
A low residual does not prove that fitted parameters are unique.

[Spectra are constraints, not fingerprints](/notes/spectra-constraints/)  
Agreement with a spectrum means consistency, not structural identification.

[Averaging destroys information irreversibly](/notes/averaging/)  
A mean does not determine the distribution that produced it.

[Simulation is not deployment evidence](/notes/simulation-not-deployment/)  
A controlled model result does not prove field performance.
